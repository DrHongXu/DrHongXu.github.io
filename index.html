Currently, Dr. Hong Xu is working as a Development Engineer for Commerical Fuel Cell Vehicles in Automotive Industry based in Munich, Germany. His academic and industrial interdisplinary interests are focused on Renewable Energy, Sustainability, Functional Materials and Equity Research.

He received his PhD with research focus on Carbon Neutral Energy Technologies from ETH Zurich in 2021, and engaged in 2 years Industrial and 4 years Academic collaborative projects for R&D of automotive hydrogen fuel cells. In 2016, he obtained EU dual master degrees in Materials Science from the Technical University of Munich, and Physics from the University of Rennes I with industrial practices at German Companies and training at French Institute. He went to university at a young age of 16 years old, and he finished the bachelor studies at Beijing Transport University with 3 patents granted in 2013. In parallel to academic research, he developed professional education as a part-time MBA fellow at the Quantic School of Business with internships at Venture Capital and Securities Companies in China.
